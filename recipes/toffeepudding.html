Sticky Toffee Pudding

Recipe taken from Spoon Fork Bacon

Description

If you are not familiar with traditional toffee pudding, it is comprised of a soft, fluffy sponge cake made with dates, covered in rich toffee sauce, and topped with a vanilla custard or cream. This delicious recipe is very similar to that classic comfort food favourite, with a few minor additions. Cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves add a level of depth and flavour. This recipe also swaps out the richer, and denser custard for a lighter whipped cream. What you are left with is a fluffy, chewy, moist cake with the delicious decadence of a classic toffee sauce.

Ingredients

Sponge Cake:

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
  • 24 dates, pitted and chopped (5oz)
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 1/2 cups dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Toffee Sauce:

  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
